Our Approach

A child born into the bottom 40% of U.S. income has a 60% chance of remaining there as an adult. Organization is working to rewrite that story with a four-pronged approach.

Delivering Economic Mobility for Students

Fixing the college-to-career transition so students secure high-trajectory jobs.

Maximizing Career Trajectories

Ensuring individuals realize their full potential and multiply their impact.

Cultivating Entrepreneurs & High-Growth Businesses

Empowering leaders to scale ventures that create jobs and opportunity for others.

Strengthening Employer Talent Practices

Enhancing employer competitiveness while expanding economic opportunity.

"From growing up in public housing to helping build it, I want to use my story to help others shape theirs."

Strengthening Employer Practices

With most Americans working for large companies, employers are one of the most powerful levers for expanding economic opportunity at scale. Organization's analytics, benchmarking, and coaching help major employers elevate the rigor of their talent practices.

  • Strengthen inclusive recruiting practices
  • Clarify the high-performance and promotion bar for all employees
  • Boost retention through employee resource group engagement
  • Expand supplier diversity and procurement practices
